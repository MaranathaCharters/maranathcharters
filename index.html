Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers. We’re always happy to talk through details for your specific trip.

How many people can stay overnight?
We’re set up for up to six overnight guests in private cabins. This keeps the trip comfortable and relaxed for everyone.
Do I need boating experience?
None at all. Your professional captain and crew handle the sailing, docking, anchoring, and navigation. You’re welcome to help or learn, but it’s never required.
Is food included?
We offer flexible provisioning. We can either stock the boat to your preferences or handle full meal planning for you. Provisioning is priced per guest per day and tailored to your group.
What about seasickness?
Catamarans tend to be more stable than monohulls. We also work with your captain to choose routes and anchorages that prioritize comfort whenever conditions allow.
Can we customize the itinerary?
Absolutely. Every multi-day charter is customized around your preferences, within the realities of weather, tides, and travel distance for your dates.
Is there Wi-Fi onboard?
Connectivity options can be arranged depending on your route, but we encourage guests to treat time aboard as a chance to unplug when possible.
Liability waivers & policies

Before boarding, all guests will be asked to review and sign a standard Guest Liability Waiver & Assumption of Risk form. This is normal for yacht charters and helps ensure a clear, safe experience for everyone.

Full terms, cancellation policies, and sample waivers can be provided upon request or will be linked directly from your booking confirmation.
