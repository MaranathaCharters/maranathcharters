Why Maranatha?

Because you love the idea of sailing New England — but you’d rather not give up space, comfort, or privacy to do it.

Maranatha is a 53’ Royal Cape sailing catamaran with a generous beam and a layout that feels surprisingly like a modern condo. That means:

  • A huge, sheltered cockpit for long dinners and unhurried mornings
  • Private cabins, real mattresses, and space for everyone to spread out
  • Separate lounging areas for reading, napping, chatting, or just watching the horizon

If traditional monohulls ever felt a little too tight, you’ll notice the difference the moment you step aboard.

Feels like a vacation home

With five cabins and wide living spaces, Maranatha feels more like a small waterfront condo than a typical sailboat interior.

Stable, comfortable ride

Catamarans are more stable than many monohulls. You get the magic of sailing with less heeling and more comfort underway and at anchor.

Made for multi-day trips

She’s set up for easy living over days at a time — with air conditioning, generous freshwater capacity, and real storage.

Just six overnight guests

We keep things intentionally small and personal: one boat, up to six guests, and a professional crew focused entirely on your trip.
